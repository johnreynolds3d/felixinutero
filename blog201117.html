Design

20 NOVEMBER 2017

I've been looking through a lot of templates for ideas to spice up the ol' website, and I also did a bit of reading about Design. I knew that Steve Jobs was a great admirer of Dieter Rams — and I've always liked the stuff he did with Braun — so I'm going to have a crack at summarising his 'Ten Principles for Good Design':

  1. Innovative
  2. Practical
  3. Beautiful
  4. Informative
  5. Unobtrusive
  6. Honest
  7. Enduring
  8. Thorough
  9. Environmentally-friendly
  10. Simple

These remind me of Japanese art — in particular, the poetry of Bashõ, and Sumi-e paintings.

I also decided to replace the whites and greys on my site with some tasty porcelain tones. Shades of Delft, methinks; perhaps some Wedgwood would be nice?
